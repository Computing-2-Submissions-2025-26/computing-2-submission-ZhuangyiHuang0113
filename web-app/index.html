TEMPLE TRAIL

LOCAL MULTIPLAYER

2-4 explorers share one screen. Pass the device when prompted so every decision stays secret.

No room code or network connection is required.

HOW TO PLAY

01

BEGIN THE ADVENTURE

The adventure unfolds over five rounds. At the beginning of each round, every explorer who dares to enter the cave must offer one blue gem as a deposit. Then, one ancient treasure card numbered 1-5 is secretly added to that round's deck.

02

SPLIT TREASURE

The 15 treasure cards are worth 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 5, 7, 7, 9, 11, 11, 13, 14, 15 and 17. Divide the value equally among explorers still inside; the remainder stays on that card.

03

CHOOSE SECRETLY

After every reveal, choose KEEP EXPLORING or RETURN TO TENT within eight seconds. In local multiplayer, pass the device and let each active explorer choose privately.

04

WATCH FOR DANGER

There are 15 danger cards: three each of fire, landslide, snakes, spiders and mummy. A second matching danger ends the round; explorers still inside lose their loot and deposit, and one matching danger card is removed from later rounds.

05

CLAIM RELICS

There are five numbered relic cards with no separate powers. A relic can be claimed only when exactly one explorer returns. This edition scores its printed number x 10: relics 1-5 are worth 10, 20, 30, 40 and 50 points.

06

REVEAL THE TENTS

Safe loot, recovered deposits and claimed relics stay hidden in each tent. After round five, all totals are revealed and ranked. The explorer with the highest score wins.

DECK: 15 TREASURE + 15 DANGER + 5 RELICS
