GREEDY LITTLE EXPLORERS

LOCAL MULTIPLAYER

2-4 explorers share one screen. Pass the device when prompted so every decision stays secret.

No room code or network connection is required.

CAVE GAME QUICK GUIDE

01

GAME GOAL

5 rounds.

Explore the cave.

Collect treasure and relics.

Return safely to score.

Highest score wins.

02

CARD POOL

35 cards total:

15 Treasure + 15 Danger + 5 Relics.

Each round: draw 20 cards from Treasure & Danger onto the route.

Relic values: 1=10, 2=20, 3=30, 4=40, 5=50 pts.

03

MOVE & REVEAL

Start with 4 blue gems.

Pay 1 gem to enter a round.

It is returned only if the explorer returns safely to the tent.

Roll the white dice.

Move and reveal the card.

Cards can be: Treasure / Danger / Relic.

Loot is safe only after returning.

04

CHOOSE YOUR FATE

After each reveal: choose within 8 seconds.

KEEP EXPLORING: go deeper and risk more.

RETURN TO TENT: leave safely and save loot.

One returner takes all revealed treasure.

Multiple returners share equally.

Leftovers stay on the route.

Relics need exactly one returner.

05

DANGER & WIN

5 danger types. 3 cards each.

First danger = warning.

Same danger again = round ends.

Players still inside lose Round Loot + Deposit.

That danger type is removed.

Maximum: 2 danger types removed.

Then all remaining dangers stay.

After 5 rounds, count saved treasure, gems, deposits, and relics.

Highest score wins.
