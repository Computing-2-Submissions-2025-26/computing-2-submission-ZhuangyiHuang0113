TEMPLE TREASURE

LOCAL MULTIPLAYER

2-4 explorers share one screen. Pass the device when prompted so every decision stays secret.

No room code or network connection is required.

HOW TO PLAY

01

GAME GOAL

The adventure lasts for five rounds.

Your goal is to collect as much treasure as possible and finish the game with the highest score.

In each round, explorers may enter the cave, collect treasure, face danger, and decide whether to keep exploring or return safely to their tent.

02

ENTER THE CAVE

Before a local game begins, players may enter their names and choose from eight character designs.

At the beginning of the game, each player starts with four blue gems as their starting fund. These four points count in the final score from the start.

Every explorer who wants to enter a round must pay one blue gem as a deposit.

The deposit is deducted at the start of the round. It is returned only if the explorer returns safely to the tent. If the explorer does not return safely, the deposit is lost.

After this, one ancient relic card numbered 1-5 is secretly added to the deck for that round.

The deeper you go, the greater the reward, but also the greater the danger.

03

MOVE AND REVEAL

The full card pool contains 15 treasure cards, 15 danger cards, and 5 relic cards.

In each round, the 20 cards on the board are randomly drawn from the treasure and danger cards, with that round's relic card added to the deck.

When your explorer's tent is highlighted, click the white dice.

Your explorer moves forward by the number shown on the dice. Then, the landing card is revealed:

  • If the card is hidden, it flips open.
  • If the card is already open, it is shown again.

Landing cards may reveal treasure, relics, or danger.

04

UNDERSTAND YOUR PACK

Your pack shows what you currently have and what is already safe.

SAVED TOTAL
Your safe score, including the four starting gems, secured treasure, safely claimed relics, and any lost deposits.
ARTIFACTS
Special relic bonuses you have collected. These are included once they are safely claimed.
CURRENT ROUND
This round's gain. It can show -1 if the explorer loses the entry deposit to danger.

In local multiplayer, the pack may stay private until it is that player's choice step.

05

CHOOSE YOUR FATE

After each card is revealed, you have 8 seconds to choose:

KEEP EXPLORING
Continue deeper into the cave and risk collecting more treasure.

RETURN TO TENT
Leave the cave safely, recover your deposit, and secure the treasure currently on the route.

In local multiplayer mode, QUIT GAME removes that player from the rest of the match.

06

SHARE RETURNED TREASURE

If only one person leaves, the person takes all the treasures revealed on the board.

If multiple players choose to return to the tent at the same time, they share the treasure currently on the route equally.

Any treasure that cannot be divided equally remains on the route for later players to claim.

Choose carefully. Staying longer may bring greater rewards, but it may also cost you everything from that round.

07

WATCH FOR DANGER

There are 15 danger cards in total:

  • 3 Fire cards
  • 3 Landslide cards
  • 3 Snake cards
  • 3 Spider cards
  • 3 Mummy cards

The first danger card of a type is only a warning.

If a second matching danger card appears in the same round, the round ends immediately. All explorers still inside the cave lose their Round Loot and their deposit.

After that, all three cards of that danger type are removed from the deck for later rounds.

08

CLAIM RELICS AND WIN

Relics are special bonus treasures.

A relic can only be claimed when exactly one explorer returns to the tent at that moment.

  • Relic 1: 10 points
  • Relic 2: 20 points
  • Relic 3: 30 points
  • Relic 4: 40 points
  • Relic 5: 50 points

Before a local game begins, players may enter their names and choose from eight characters.

After the fifth round ends, all secured treasure and safely claimed relics are counted. The explorer with the highest score wins.

09

TREASURE CARD VALUES

There are 15 treasure cards in the full card pool.

The treasure cards are worth:

  • 1, 2, 3, 4
  • 5, 5
  • 7, 7
  • 9
  • 11, 11
  • 13, 14, 15, 17

Cards with larger numbers are rarer and more valuable, so revealing one can make the route much more tempting.

Treasure is shown with gems: blue gems are worth 1, black gems are worth 5, and gold gems are worth 10.
